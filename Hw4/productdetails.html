Product Details

Matte: Provides a completely shine-free, velvety finish. It is highly pigmented and offers the most staying power, but can feel drying.Satin: A middle-ground finish that is semi-matte with a subtle sheen. It feels more comfortable and hydrating than traditional matte formulas.Cream: Known for a smooth, emollient texture with a soft glow. It is one of the most common everyday formulas because it feels lightweight and nourishing. Glossy: Offers high-shine and a reflective "wet" look. Glosses often make lips appear fuller but require frequent reapplication.Sheer: Translucent and subtle, providing just a wash of colour. These are often packed with moisturizing oils.
Shade Variety: The collection generally offers a mix of everyday neutrals, romantic pinks, classic reds, and deep berry or burgundy tones.Matte Finish: Each liquid lipstick dries to a velvet matte finish that is formulated to be comfortable and lightweight without drying out the lips.Long-Lasting Formula: The high-pigmentation formula provides smudge-resistant and transfer-proof color intended for all-day wear.Easy Application: Features a precision applicator designed for smooth, one-stroke coverage.Safe Ingredients: Many variants are listed as cruelty-free and paraben-free, often infused with moisturizing ingredients like Vitamin E to maintain lip softness.
